The following discourse pertains to the system architecture and design decisions.

The selection of the airframe is a pivotal component in the overall design process.
The selection of a lightweight EPP glider was made on the basis of its long flight time, stability, and capacity for the carriage of additional electronics.

The selection of the flight controller is a pivotal decision.
The F4 V3 flight controller was selected on the basis of its compatibility with INAV and its integrated sensors, including a barometer.

## GPS Integration
The BE220 M8N GPS module facilitates the measurement of speed, the collection of navigation data, and the facilitation of Return-To-Home functionality.

The following text concerns a FPV system.
The integration of camera and video transmitter into a unified unit by an all-in-one FPV camera serves to streamline the installation process.

## Safety Design
Failsafe logic is implemented through INAV to return the aircraft to the launch point if radio signal or battery voltage is lost.
